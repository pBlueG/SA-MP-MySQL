row::row (4 of 4 overloads)

Constructs a row from a row_view.

Synopsis 
row(
    row_view r);
Exception safety

Strong guarantee. Internal allocations may throw.

Object lifetimes

*this lifetime will be independent of r's (the contents of r will be copied into *this).

Complexity

Linear on r.size().
